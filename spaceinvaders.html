Space Invaders

made with Scratch

In the first week of auditing CS50, Harvard's Intro to CS course, I was tasked with creating a game in Scratch, a graphical programming language created at M.I.T. I was feeling ambitious, so I decided to create Space Invaders, a nostalgic game from my childhood. I wanted to replicate as many features from the original game as possible including the destroyable barriers, the randomly appearing red bonus ship, and the overall speed and feel of playing. It took a ton of trial and error to get it to work but I think I was able to come up with a decent rendition of the game.
